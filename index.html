About

"Hello, I'm Carl; and I love exploring the web while expanding my skills to build things for it. My journey as a developer started when I was in junior high school. A tutorial on how to use HTML to display pictures with labels on my browser sparked my interest in this job. After that, I became completely hooked on the world of computers, and the rest is history.

Over the years, I've grown my interest in coding by learning about information technology, but mostly by building websites with JavaScript and CSS to challenge myself. This allowed me to discover more things that I never thought I would and do a lot more I never thought I could.

I've only recently discovered my love for web development and the beauty of making useful websites like the ones in this portfolio. I'm presently looking for a junior developer job to finally get my career off the ground and learn more among professionals."
